Exoskeleton

2023 Spring


Using thick aluminum wire, Yejin Han created armor inspired by seashells as a sculptural accessory. This project shows the core aesthetic of Yejin Han’s artistic style, which is silently impactful. The photos of the work were taken during the summer of 2024 after Yejin gained the knowledge and technique of making garments and creating the dress that can go with the accessory.
