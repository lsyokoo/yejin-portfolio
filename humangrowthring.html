Human Growth Ring

2023 Fall


The first ever fashion project that Yejin Han worked on after coming to Parsons School of Art and Design focuses on the beauty of naturally growing old. The project studies societal perspectives towards the human life progress of growing old and how people can change their perspective and embrace the process of aging.
