Studio Knotique

2024 Spring


Combining the word 'knot' and 'antiquity', Studio Knotique is an imaginary brand that dives into the study of Korean traditional knots and antique housewares. Starting from her grandmother's old closet with mother-of-pearl lacquerware, Yejin Han dives into the study of silhouettes in traditional Korean housewares and the ways they can be translated into garments. She adds up the quality of the detail of each product by incorporating traditional knots into her work.
